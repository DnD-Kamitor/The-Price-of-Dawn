# Serah Tallon — Character Sheet Summary

**Class:** Paladin 4 (Oath of Devotion) / Sorcerer 4 (Draconic Bloodline) (gestalt)
**Species:** Wood Elf
**Background:** Wayfarer (traveler)
**Player:** unknown (D&D Beyond: wayfahrer)
**Alignment:** Lawful Good

## Appearance
Youngish wood elf with bronze skin covered in dragonlike scales. Bronze hair, oxidized bronze eyes. Traveller's clothes in bronze/earth tones, bunch of necklaces with pendants. Travels with her Elk companion: **Brann**.

## Stats (Level 4)
- STR 8 (-1) | DEX 16 (+3) | CON 14 (+2) | INT 8 (-1) | WIS 14 (+2) | CHA 18 (+4)
- AC 19 (with shield) | HP 40 (4d10) | Speed 35 ft.
- Proficiency +2 | Spellcasting: CHA, DC 14, +6 attack

## Combat
- Rapier: +5, 1d8+3 piercing (Vex, Ruby of the War Mage attuned)
- Light Crossbow: +5, 1d8+3 piercing (Slow)
- Dagger: +5, 1d4+3 piercing (Nick)
- Whip: +5, 1d4+3 slashing (Slow, Reach)

## Key Paladin Features
- **Lay on Hands:** 20 HP pool (restore HP or cure disease/poison)
- **Channel Divinity (2/short rest):** Divine Sense (detect celestials/fiends/undead as BA), Sacred Weapon (+CHA to attacks for 1 min)
- **Paladin's Smite:** Divine Smite always prepared; can smite without spell slot 1×/no short rest
- **Devotion Spells:** always prepared — Protection from Evil and Good, Shield of Faith, Hold Person, etc.

## Key Sorcerer Features
- **Innate Sorcery:** once per day, bonus action to empower spells
- **Font of Magic:** 4 Sorcery Points
- **Metamagic:** Quickened Spell (2 SP), Twinned Spell (1 SP)
- **Draconic Resilience:** +1 HP per level (included), AC 13 without armor (Bronze dragon bloodline)

## Spells Known/Prepared
**Cantrips:** Druidcraft, Mage Hand, Message, Mind Sliver, Minor Illusion, Sorcerous Burst
**1st:** Chromatic Orb, Command, Divine Favor, Divine Smite, Find Steed, Longstrider, Protection from Evil and Good, Shield, Shield of Faith
**2nd:** Alter Self, Dragon's Breath, Hold Person, Mirror Image, Misty Step, Web

## Feats
- **Lucky:** 2 Luck Points (reroll attack/check/save or force enemy reroll)

## Wood Elf Traits
- Darkvision 60 ft., Fey Ancestry, Trance
- Elven Lineage (Wood Elf)

## Backstory
Left at a wayshrine as a young girl, wearing a necklace with a dragonlike talon (origin of surname "Tallon"). Now travels the road in service of her protector deity **the Wanderer**, protecting travellers and searching for her family. Her Elk companion is named **Brann**.

*Note: Serah appears in Ixa's backstory as the woman who led the raid on the Halcyon Crucible and rescued Ixa. This is a deliberate player-created connection between the two characters.*

## Equipment

## Languages
Common, Elvish, Draconic
