## Key Fighter Features (Champion)
- **Improved Critical:** Crits on 19-20
- **Remarkable Athlete:** Advantage on Initiative and Athletics checks; after a crit, move half speed without provoking OA
- **Action Surge:** 1/short rest — take one additional action
- **Second Wind:** 3/long rest (1 regained per short rest) — 1d10+4 HP as Bonus Action; can also use to add 1d10 to a failed ability check
- **Great Weapon Fighting feat:** Reroll 1s and 2s on damage dice with two-handed weapons
- **Sentinel feat:** OA when enemy Disengages or hits someone else within 5 ft; hit creature's Speed = 0
- **Tough feat:** +8 max HP, +2 per level

## Key Bard Features (College of Glamour)
- **Bardic Inspiration:** 2/long rest — Bonus Action, give ally within 60 ft a d6 to add to a failed D20 Test within 1 hour
- **Beguiling Magic:** 1/long rest (or spend Bardic Inspiration) — after casting Enchantment/Illusion spell, Charm or Frighten a creature (DC 12 WIS save, 1 min)
- **Mantle of Inspiration:** Spend Bardic Inspiration — up to 2 creatures within 60 ft gain Temp HP = 2× roll, then can use Reaction to move full speed without OA
- **Jack of All Trades:** +1 (half prof) to skill checks without proficiency
- **Expertise:** Two skill proficiencies at double proficiency (Persuasion + one other based on build)

## Backstory (player description)
Farmer who does everything by hand — no machines, no tools, hence the physique. Heard a city had no sun. Found that strange from a farming perspective. Came to find out how they're growing crops without sunlight. Wants to help bring back the sun so people can farm normally. Brought seeds as a gift.

## Campaign Hooks
- Varenhold's food crisis is his domain — he understands supply collapse from the ground up
- Glamour Bard = unexpectedly magnetic for a Goliath farmer; the party face in moments of community/crowd work
- Seeds as a prop: genuine hope gesture, lands hard before the ritual cost is revealed
- Natural connection to Cormac Drell (dock logistics) and Harrow Seld (parish farming collapse)
- When he learns the ritual costs lives: his entire motivation (fix farming) is now in direct tension with his nature (can't casually choose who dies)
