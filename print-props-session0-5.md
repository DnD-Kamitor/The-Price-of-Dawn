#### The Vault Sealing Document — *Give if Investigation DC 16 passed searching the vault workbenches*

*Old parchment, sealed in a stone recess behind a loose workbench stone. The wax seal is the same symbol as the vault door's central mark. Writing is formal and academic, approximately 50 years old.*

---

**DOCUMENT OF FORMAL SEALING**
**Second Restoration Attempt — Final Record**
**Year of the Amber Accord, Third Month**

We, the undersigned scholars of the Restoration Working Group, do hereby seal this vault in acknowledgment of our second failure to restore natural daylight to Varenhold.

We do not seal it in shame. We seal it as a record.

**The order of our understanding is as follows:**

First, we acknowledge what was: the disruption that began everything — the failure of the original ritual, the breaking of the natural cycle. We do not blame those who tried. We honour their courage and name their error honestly: *Hagalaz. The hailstorm that cannot be recalled.*

Second, we acknowledge what drove us here: the necessity of acting when living memory of the original method still existed — the need of a people without their sun, the need of a city that built a thousand lanterns to replace one star. *Nauthiz. The need that cannot be ignored.*

Third, we acknowledge what we attempted: the correction. We believed we had identified the calibration error. We had. Our method was sound. Our application was wrong. The controlled flame was applied with too much force and too little patience. *Kenaz. The smith's fire, mishandled.*

Fourth, we name the goal that remains unreached: the sun. It has not returned. We believe it can. We believe the next attempt, made with full knowledge of our two failures, will succeed where we could not. *Sowilo. The sun that waits.*

**Let the one who opens this door know the order in which we understood our failure. It is the same order in which this door must be opened.**

*Signed and sealed by: [three signatures, names partially illegible — a V., a M., and a third beginning with E.]*
:::

#### Calibration Manual Excerpt — *Give if Investigation DC 14 passed on vault workbenches*

*A hand-copied technical document, moderately worn. Academic handwriting. Several margin annotations in a different, later hand.*

---

**PRIMER ANCHOR CALIBRATION — OPERATIONAL GUIDE**
*Copied from the original working notes of the Second Attempt*

The Primer Anchor operates through four calibration rings, each resonating with a specific paired energy. The plinths built around the anchor perimeter each house a two-layer adjustment mechanism — an inner and outer rotating ring. Both must be correctly aligned before the stabilization check can succeed.

**Ring One — The Bell**
The Bell ring governs the human voice carrying divine intent — the prayer that reaches. Align the inner ring to *Mannaz* (Man) and the outer ring to *Ansuz* (God). The bell speaks when the hand of man carries the word of god.

**Ring Two — The Chain**
The Chain ring governs sustained effort across time — the binding of immediate need to long patience. Align the inner ring to *Nauthiz* (Need) and the outer ring to *Jera* (Harvest, the turning year). The chain holds when necessity grasps the turning year.

**Ring Three — The Eye**
The Eye ring governs revealed knowledge — the hidden becoming known through trust. Align the inner ring to *Ehwaz* (Horse, trust, partnership) and the outer ring to *Perthro* (the Dice Cup, the hidden). The eye sees when trust opens what is hidden.

**Ring Four — The Flame**
The Flame ring governs directed force — the giant's power made purposeful. Align the inner ring to *Thurisaz* (Giant, forceful disruption channelled) and the outer ring to *Sowilo* (Sun, the goal). The flame arrives when the giant force is pointed at the sun.

*Margin note in different hand:* "Ring Three took us four attempts. Ehwaz is easy to confuse with Raidho. They are not the same. Ehwaz is partnership. Raidho is travel. The Eye cares about trust, not movement."

*Second margin note:* "Ring Four — do not linger. Once Thurisaz and Sowilo are aligned, make the check immediately. The Flame ring is volatile."
:::

#### Makers' Failure Analysis — *Give if History DC 14 passed on vault wall inscriptions*

*Carved directly into the east wall of the vault in tight, urgent script. Less formal than the Sealing Document. Written last.*

---

**POST-ATTEMPT ANALYSIS — FOR WHOEVER COMES NEXT**

We are writing this quickly. The vault needs to be sealed before morning.

The second attempt failed. We know why. We are leaving this record because the *why* matters more than the fact of the failure, and we do not trust the Sealing Document's formal language to communicate urgency clearly enough.

**The failure was Kenaz.**

We understood the sequence correctly. Hagalaz, Nauthiz, Kenaz, Sowilo — disruption acknowledged, need accepted, method applied, goal affirmed. This is correct. We are confident in this.

The error was in how we used the third mark.

Kenaz is the controlled flame. The smith's fire. The torch held steady. It requires patience. It requires a sustained, deliberate burn — not an explosion. We pressed Ring Three too hard and too fast. The Flame ring, which amplifies directed force toward the goal, cannot handle an uncontrolled ignition. What should have been a measured correction became a surge. The anchor overloaded.

**The correction is not in the sequence. The sequence is right. The correction is in the application of the third step.**

Hold Kenaz steady. Do not rush it. The third ring is the one that fails people who are certain they understand it. Understanding it is not enough. Patience with it is the requirement.

We believe the third attempt will succeed. We are not alive to make it. Whoever is: the sequence is in the door. The calibration method is in the manual on the workbench. The failure is written here.

Do not repeat our patience error with the sequence itself. Do not try to hurry the sun.

*— Unsigned. The handwriting matches the third signature on the Sealing Document.*
:::

**MY RECORD OF THE SECOND ATTEMPT SITE**
*Personal notes — M.V.*

I have been given something extraordinary.

Inside: a map of the Dawnhall on Crestwick Lane, a floor plan of a vault beneath it that I had never heard existed, a copy of a door sequence I did not know existed, and a complete copy of what the sender calls "the calibration manual." The letter said the vault was built by the same scholars who created the Dawnborn. It said the second attempt failed but was not wrong. It said the sequence is correct.

If a third attempt is made with proper preparation — with the calibration method the makers left, with the patience they said they lacked — the sun can return. I believe this. I have seen the evidence with my own eyes.

I do not know who sent me the letter. The handwriting was formal. Precise. Official. I have seen that style before in Spire documents, but I cannot imagine a Spire official sending this to me. They would want to suppress it, not share it.

*I am choosing not to investigate further. The work is more important than the source.*

*Margin note, later:* "I should have investigated the source. I know that now. But if I had, would I have come? Would any of this have happened? I do not know."
